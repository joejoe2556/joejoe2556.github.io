Step 1

Create a steam account and download the game. Go to the steam store look for Kaidro: Clan battles. Download and wait for it to appear in the library. Log into the game. Create an in-game account. Remember your username and password as you will be logging this into the Kaidro website later on.

Create an account in the Kaidro website. Now for this step you need to have a ronin wallet. I've placed a direct link to a tutorial how to create a ronin wallet below. Once that's done you congratulations you are now part of the kaidro universe.
